Welcome!

The Moscow Chamber of Commerce is a non-governmental non-profit organization established in 1991.

Our main goal is to support Moscow business and promote the development of entrepreneurship in Moscow, building effective relationships between business and government.

Today the Moscow Chamber of Commerce unites more than 3,800 companies from various segments and sectors of the Moscow economy - large, medium and small businesses, individual entrepreneurs, state, municipal and public organizations, branches of foreign companies. We are the largest association of entrepreneurs in the capital of Russia. On this site you can learn more about us and join. Contact us if you have any questions, we are happy to help!
